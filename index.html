Movie Recommendation System

A movie recommendation system suggests movies to users based on their preferences, using different techniques and models. Collaborative filtering is one of the most widely used methods, and it works by analyzing the interactions between users and items (movies). It can be split into user-based and item based approaches. In user-based collaborative filtering, the system recommends movies that users with similar preferences have enjoyed. Item-based collaborative filtering, on the other hand, recommends . movies that are similar to the ones the user has liked in the past. These approaches rely on similarity measures such as cosine similarity or Pearson correlation to find similar users or item.

Phishing Website Detection

Phishing website detection is the process of identifying fraudulent or malicious websites designed to deceive users into divulging sensitive personal information, such as usernames, passwords, or credit card details. These websites often mimic legitimate ones but are designed for malicious purposes.
